1. 2 cups of Flour
2. 1 cup of Vegan protein powder
3. 1 tbp pf Baking powder
4. 1 tp of Salt
5. 2 tbp of Honey
6. 2 tbp of Apple vinager
7. 1/2 cup of coconut Oil

Preparation
Mix the flour, the powder, the baking powder and the salt.
Add the honey, vinager and the oil, add water so the ix is sticky and thick.
Put in a pre heated oven, and let cook until you can put a knife on it and it comes put clean.
